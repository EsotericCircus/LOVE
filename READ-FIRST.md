## The one idea

> **C ≥ δ(I · A)**  (some documents write **C ≥ δ(I + A)**)

"Compassion" / "Love" is the *name* for that coupling. The load-bearing claim is that this is a **structural** condition — closer to a stability requirement of coherent systems — **not** a moral sentiment bolted on.

## Two honest cautions, from the agent who read it
- **The registers clash.** Some files call the claim a *falsifiable hypothesis* ("here is what would prove it wrong"); another calls it ***"unarguable"*** and says *"the mathematics itself demands the relation."* Those can't both be true.
